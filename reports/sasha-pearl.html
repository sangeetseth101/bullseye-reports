What to Do This Month

Your Quick Wins

Simple actions you can take this month to build real momentum for your brand.

Week 1
Name Your Differentiator Out Loud
Take the bolded sentence from the Differentiation section of this Blueprint and put it directly on your homepage and Instagram bio. Right now your site language ("soul-led," "energetics") is something every Human Design practitioner says. The line about building brands starting from a Human Design reading is the thing nobody else can say — say it.
Week 1
Rewrite Your Bio in Your Real Voice
Pull from the Voice in Action line and the "You Are" words in this Blueprint. Compare your current About page tone to how you spoke in this Blueprint — pick one page, start with your Instagram bio, and rewrite it sharper and more direct. Notice everywhere you're currently softening.
Week 2
Lead Your About Page With Your Niche, Not Your Story
Your Power Niche Statement names exactly who you serve and what changes for them. Right now, visitors to your About page get your personal story before they get clarity on whether you're for them. Put the niche statement first — then tell your story.
Week 3
Build One Piece of Content Per Pillar
You now have 5 content pillars, each with 4 ready-to-use angles. Pick one angle from each pillar and create one piece of content from it — five total. This makes the full breadth of what you do (Human Design, branding, business, content) read as one coherent system, not five separate things.
Week 4
Put the Brand Message Asset on Your Sales Page
The Brand Message Asset in this Blueprint is built to go straight onto a website or proposal. Use it as the opening of your Brand Embodiment or Big Biz Energy sales page — it names the struggle, the desired outcome, and your method in two sentences. This is the highest-leverage rewrite in this entire Blueprint, because it's the first thing a cold visitor reads.
